The "Epiphany" Proposal

To ensure efficient evolution without the cost of real re-training, I propose the Tripartite Evolutionary Loop:

1

Adaptive Persona Mutation

Failures trigger the PAC (Protocolo de Angulação Crítica). If PAC resolves the issue, the solution is baked into a new version of the Specialist's Persona automatically.

2

Autonomous Speciation

If a "Universal Operator" consistently succeeds in internal/graph, the system clones it into a GraphSpecialist with optimized context windows for that module.

3

Competitive Culling

Periodically, the Dispatcher runs "Sentinel Shadow Tasks" (A/B tests). The persona with higher token efficiency and success rate "consumes" the resources of the inferior one.
